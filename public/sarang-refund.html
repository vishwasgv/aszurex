Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 2026. Applies to paid Sarang license renewals only — the first 12 months of Sarang are free and this policy has nothing to refund during that period.

Refunds

If you renew your Sarang license and are not satisfied, email contact@aszurex.com within 14 days of the charge for a full refund. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of your request being confirmed, back to your original payment method.

Cancellations

A Sarang license is a single annual charge, not an auto-renewing subscription — there is nothing to "cancel" in the recurring-billing sense. If you don't renew when your license period ends, Sarang simply continues in its existing free-tier behavior: all your existing data stays fully accessible (viewable, printable, exportable), only creating new invoices is affected until you choose to renew.

An honest limitation, stated plainly

Sarang is built offline-first — the application does not require an internet connection to keep working, by design. This means that when we process a refund, we cannot instantly and unconditionally revoke a license the way a purely cloud-based product could. What actually happens: a refunded license is added to a list the app checks the next time it happens to be online (an optional, non-blocking check it already performs at most once a day). A device that comes online within a day or two of a refund will correctly revert to free-tier behavior; a device that stays offline for an extended period may not see that change until it next connects. We're telling you this directly rather than implying a guarantee our architecture can't fully back — if this matters to your situation, mention it when requesting a refund and we'll work with you directly.

International purchases

Renewals made outside India are processed by Lemon Squeezy as merchant of record. The refund timeline above still applies; Lemon Squeezy handles the actual transaction reversal on our instruction.

Contact

Refund requests and questions: contact@aszurex.com. Support hours: Monday–Friday, 10:00–18:00 IST.
